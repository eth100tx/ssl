## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Full company name: **Showtime Sound & Lighting**
- Abbreviated version: **SSL Inc.** (for internal/technical documents only)

### Logo Clear Space
- Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "S" in Showtime on all sides
- Never place logo on busy backgrounds without a solid backing

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not change colors outside approved palette
- Do not add effects (shadows, glows, outlines)
- Do not rotate or tilt

## Typography

### Primary Headline Font
**Bebas Neue**
- Use for: Large headlines, hero text, impactful statements
- Style: Bold, uppercase
- Letter-spacing: 0.05em
- Available: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Bebas+Neue)

### Secondary Headline Font
**Inter**
- Use for: Section headers, subheadings, UI elements
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- Available: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)

### Body Font
**DM Sans**
- Use for: Body copy, paragraphs, UI text
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- Available: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Sans)

### Font Hierarchy

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Color |

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Attributes
1. **Professional** - Technical expertise without jargon
2. **Reliable** - Confident, dependable, consistent
3. **Direct** - Clear, concise, no fluff
4. **Warm** - Approachable, human, caring

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Avoid industry jargon in customer-facing materials
- Use "we" and "you" to create connection

### Example Messaging

**Do:**
> "We ensure your event runs flawlessly—so you can focus on what matters."

**Don't:**
> "Our company provides audio-visual solutions leveraging industry-leading technology platforms."

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style
- **Lighting:** Dramatic, professional stage/event lighting
- **Subjects:** Equipment in action, crews at work, live events
- **Mood:** Professional, energetic, precise
- **Avoid:** Stock-looking poses, cluttered backgrounds, poor lighting

### Color Treatment
- Images may have slight warm or cool color grading
- Maintain high contrast
- Dark/moody backgrounds preferred
- Accent lighting in gold or cyan encouraged

### Icons
- Use simple, line-based icons
- Stroke weight: 2px
- Color: Stage Gold, Electric Cyan, or Primary Text
- Style: Modern, minimal, technical

### Website
- Dark theme primary
- Stage Gold for CTAs and important actions
- Electric Cyan for secondary actions and links
- Minimum button size: 44x44px for touch targets

### Social Media
- Profile image: Logo mark on dark background
- Cover images: Event photography with gold/cyan accents
- Post style: Dark backgrounds, high contrast, gold accents

### Email Marketing
- Dark header with logo
- Light body for readability (if needed)

- Footer in dark with light text
